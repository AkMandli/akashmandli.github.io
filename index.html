About Me

I work at the intersection of strategy and execution.

I'm a Business Analyst who's comfortable in a client pitch one afternoon and deep in a BRD the next morning. My career has been built around one core belief: requirements written with precision save weeks of engineering time and thousands in rework costs.

At X-Byte Enterprise Solutions, I ran complete pre-sales cycles — from RFP analysis and solution scoping to client presentations and project handoffs. At Bytes Technolabs, I deepened my delivery-side practice with structured documentation, stakeholder interviews, and agile coordination across international teams.

I work best with IT service firms, digital product studios, and consulting organizations who need someone that can hold the client relationship and own the documentation layer simultaneously.
